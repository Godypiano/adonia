About Us

Our company based in Tanzania (Dar es salaam E.A).

Originally established in the year 2000 by our esteemed mentor and senior director Shyaka Vianney this company strives to be the very best in the business. As fruits of his continuous and non-stop efforts from the year 2008 to 2014 the company was registered with the TRA (Tanzania Revenue Authority) with the tax payer ID 125-809-235.We mainly deal with the clearing and forwarding of imported freight goods to our clients in their selected destinations.
